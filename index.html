My Cat Roger

Hello everyone. This is my cat Roger. He was given to my by a close family friend last April. Having a cat is awesome. They are kind of like people in a funny way.
Roger is a great cat, he has a really relaxed personality and he loves to play. He does all kinds of adorable things like trying to drink water from the faucet,
rolling around on the floor when he wants attention, and running around at 4 in the morning every day for some god damned reason.

Although Roger wakes me up early in the morning
and has scratched the leather off every arm rest in my apartment,
he is still a pretty cool cat. Maybe even the best.

Thank you for taking the time to read and learn about my cat.
I hope he is as cute to you as he is to me
and that the picture of him brings you as much joy as it does to me.
